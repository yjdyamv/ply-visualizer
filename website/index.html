Camera Conventions

Control Type

Rotation Center Behavior

When double-clicking to set rotation center:

Move Camera: Camera slides on view plane to center clicked point. Keep Camera: Only rotation target changes, camera stays in place. Keep Distance: Camera moves to maintain same distance from new center.

Color & Lighting

Gamma affects original vertex colors. Unlit PLY ignores scene lights. Choose Normal or Flat lighting for scene illumination.

Shading options are only effecting PLY files with faces.
